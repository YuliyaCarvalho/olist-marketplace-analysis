# Business Questions Overview

This project investigates the Olist marketplace through four analytical lenses:

1. Marketplace Growth
2. Customer Behavior
3. Operations & Logistics
4. Profitability & Risk  




### 01_marketplace_growth/
│
├── **q01_gmv_trends**
│   What is Olist's total GMV for delivered orders, and how has monthly revenue trended?
│
├── **q02_order_volume_trends**
│   How many orders were placed over time, and how does monthly order volume relate to GMV?
│
├── **q03_delivery_reliability_trends**
│   How does delivery performance vary over time, and are there months with higher non-delivery?
│
├── **q04_seller_product_concentration**
│   How concentrated are GMV and orders across sellers and products?
│
└── **q05_category_gmv_mix**
    Which product categories drive most GMV and orders, and how stable is the mix?



### 02_customer_behavior/
│
├── **q06_repeat_customer_share**
│   How many customers are one-time vs repeat buyers, and how much GMV comes from repeat customers?
│
├── **q07_repeat_customer_unit_economics**
│   How do AOV, margin per order, and acquisition/retention costs differ between one-time and repeat customers?
│
├── **q08_customer_order_distribution**
│   How many orders does the average one-time vs repeat customer place, and how skewed is behavior?
│
├── **q09_repeat_customer_demographics**
│   How do demographics and purchase characteristics differ across repeat-purchase segments?
│
└── **q10_category_mix_by_repeat_segment**
    How does product category GMV differ between one-time, light-repeat, and heavy-repeat customers?


### 03_operations_and_logistics/
│
├── **q11_delivery_speed_vs_repeat_rate**
│   Do faster deliveries correlate with higher repeat-purchase behaviour?
│
├── **q15_operational_drivers_of_reviews**
│   Which operational factors (delivery delays, freight cost, payment type, seller performance) impact reviews and revenue?
│
├── **q16_holiday_delay_impact**
│   Do delivery delays during holidays have a stronger impact on satisfaction?
│
├── **q17_geographic_delivery_performance**
│   Which states, cities, or routes experience the worst delivery performance?
│
└── **q18_product_size_vs_logistics**
    How do product dimensions and weight affect delivery performance and freight costs?



### 04_profitability_and_risk/
│
├── **q12_reviews_vs_repeat_rate**
│   Are higher review scores associated with higher repeat-purchase behaviour?
│
├── **q13_revenue_by_repeat_segment**
│   How much revenue and profit do different repeat-purchase segments generate?
│
└── **q14_churn_by_review_score**
    Which review groups are most likely to churn?
